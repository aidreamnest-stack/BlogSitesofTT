To be a man

by Kizz Daniel

About the Song

"To be a man" by Kizz Daniel explores the struggles, determination, and emotional journey of modern manhood. The lyrics capture the relentless grind and sacrifices that come with ambition, alongside personal reflection on life and relationships.

Lyrics Analysis & Insight

The song explores the challenges of manhood and perseverance. Kizz Daniel delivers a powerful reflection on working hard, overcoming obstacles, and staying true to oneself. The repeated motifs emphasize resilience, personal growth, and the balance between ambition and emotional well-being.
