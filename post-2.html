Love Across Continents

by Nellie Arts

About the Song

"Love Across Continents" is a heartfelt song celebrating love that transcends distance and borders. Through vivid imagery and poetic lyrics, Nellie Arts captures the magic of a connection that exists across oceans and cultures. The rhythm of the song mirrors the emotional pulse of love itself — universal, uniting, and timeless.

Lyrics

We found love beneath a sky of quiet stars, No storybook, just truth in your eyes, And suddenly, the world felt whole again.

One world, one heart — beating in tune, Across every ocean, I’m drawn to you. Different lands, but love’s the same, One fire, one song, one name.

Every sunrise paints your name in light, The world turns gentle when you smile, You are the calm that love becomes.

Your flaws are verses I hold close, Your touch — the place time fades to still, Kiss me once, and forever begins again.

From every shore, beneath one sky, Love speaks in tones that never die. No borders, no lines, just hearts in rhyme — One world, one heart, one time.
